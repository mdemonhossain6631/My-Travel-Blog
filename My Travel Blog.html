About My Travel

I Love Travelling.I’m Emon, an award winning independent traveller writing about my visits to countries near and far. I research destinations myself and that’s part of the fun – visiting the local library and selecting some travel guides, looking things up online and, of course, all the amazing tips I discover from fellow travel bloggers.

Traveling is one of the most enriching experiences in life. It allows me to expore new places, meet different people, and learn about various cultures and traditions. whether it's a peaceful trip to the countryside or an exciting adventure in a busy city, every jounrney brings fresh memories and valuable lessons. Traveling also helps me relax and take a break from my daily routine. It opens my mind and broadens my perspective on the world. For me, every trip is not just about the destination, but also about the journey itself.
